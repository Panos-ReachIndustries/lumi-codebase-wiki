# Ledger

A **ledger** is the append-only record of one experiment run — what the arbiter saw, what it decided, and when. Used for audit, replay, and analysis.

Sample ledger files: `Lumi-AI-Continuous/ledgers/ledger-*.json`.
Sample top-level: `Lumi-AI-Continuous/ledger-6068803a-b61f-4888-aa84-3d1a189a8992.json`.
